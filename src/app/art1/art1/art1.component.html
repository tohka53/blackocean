Hello! My name is Jonathan Palacios. Better known as Jony, I've been a tattoo artist for 5 years, graduated with a degree in Fine Arts from the National University of El Salvador. From a young age, I felt fascination for art in most of its expressions and branches. Drawing and painting being my main interest, with the support of my parents I took some drawing classes at the age of 10. Later, I became part of some artistic collectives that aimed to show art from a personal point of view. My influences in drawing come from the old masters of classical art. Like Rembrandt, Claude Monet among others.
