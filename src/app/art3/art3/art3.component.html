My name is Rocío Martínez, known by many as Roco, Salvadoran and tattoo artist since 2018. Graduate of the School of Arts and Design at the University of El Salvador.

I really enjoy drawing and painting, I've been doing it since I was very young and currently I have the privilege of doing it on people's skin.

Although my style is still evolving, I really enjoy tattooing in color or blackwork, whether in illustrative style, new school, art nouveau and engraved art.
