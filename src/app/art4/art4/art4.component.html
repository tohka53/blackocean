International tattoo artist and founder of Paradise Tattoo Antigua Guatemala and Black Ocean located in El Salvador. With more than 10 years of experience in the artistic world of tattooing. Born in El Salvador and Guatemalan based.

I describe myself as a talkative person, I enjoy a good talk during a session. I thank all my clients for trusting my work.
